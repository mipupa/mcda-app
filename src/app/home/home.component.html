What we mean by Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis ?

Well, simply explained. MCDA is a way of helping decision-makers rationally choose between multiple options where there are several conflicting objectives. It is usually used when:

  • there are a mix of criteria that cannot be obviously compared
  • there are multiple stakeholders perspectives that affect the decision being made
  • other approaches are not suitable
